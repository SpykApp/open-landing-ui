SCHEDULING

Smart Scheduling That Works for Everyone

AI-powered scheduling that considers employee preferences, skills, certifications, and labor laws to create optimal schedules in minutes.

  • Drag-and-drop interface - Build schedules visually with an intuitive calendar view
  • Auto-scheduling - Let AI create optimized schedules based on your rules
  • Shift templates - Save and reuse common schedule patterns
  • Conflict detection - Automatic alerts for overtime, double-booking, and compliance issues
TIME TRACKING

Accurate Time Tracking, Anywhere

Multiple clock-in options with GPS verification and biometric support eliminate time theft and ensure accurate records.

  • Mobile clock-in - Employees can clock in from their phones with GPS verification
  • Geofencing - Set boundaries for where employees can clock in
  • Photo verification - Optional selfie capture to prevent buddy punching
  • Break tracking - Automatic break reminders and tracking for compliance
PAYROLL

Payroll Processing Made Easy

Process payroll in minutes with automatic calculations, tax compliance, and direct deposit support.

  • Automatic calculations - Overtime, differentials, and deductions computed automatically
  • Tax compliance - Federal, state, and local taxes calculated and filed
  • Direct deposit - Pay employees directly to their bank accounts
  • Pay stubs - Digital pay stubs accessible via the employee portal
COMPLIANCE

Stay Compliant, Automatically

Never miss a certification expiration or labor law violation with proactive compliance monitoring and alerts.

  • Certification tracking - Monitor licenses, certifications, and training requirements
  • Labor law compliance - Automatic enforcement of break rules, overtime limits, and more
  • Audit trails - Complete history of all schedule changes and time edits
  • Expiration alerts - Get notified before credentials expire
