OUR STORY

Built by People Who Understand Workforce Challenges

StaffShell was born from frustration with outdated, fragmented workforce management tools.

In 2018, our founders—former HR directors and operations managers—set out to build the platform they wished they had. They understood the pain of juggling spreadsheets, paper timesheets, and disconnected systems.

Today, StaffShell serves over 5,000 companies worldwide, from small restaurants to enterprise healthcare organizations. Our mission remains the same: make workforce management simple, accurate, and fair for everyone.
